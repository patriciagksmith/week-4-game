Crystals Collector

Game Directions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly selected number. You lose if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The game will automatically change the values of each crystal at the start of each new game
